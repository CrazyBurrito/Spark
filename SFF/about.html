About Us

SFF is a dedicated, welcoming community for anyone with an interest in Science Fiction, Fantasy, and/or gaming. We exist to plan awesome events, hold entertaining meetings, and help foster community here at CU Boulder.

We have regular meetings Tuesdays 7:00-11:00 PM and Saturdays 2:00-11:00 PM usually in UMC 335! For up to date information, like this page and/or ask to be added to the email list!

We play board games, video games and tabletop RPGs at meetings, as well as watch SF&F TV shows/movies! Everyone is welcome to attend - no experience or knowledge is necessary! We'll teach you how to play any game you want :)

This academic year we will also be hosting some incredibly awesome events! Stay tuned for more information!
